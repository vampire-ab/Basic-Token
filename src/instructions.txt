To compile and deploy the contract using Remix:

1. Open Remix (https://remix.ethereum.org/) and paste the contract code into the Solidity compiler.
2. Click the "Compile" button to compile the contract.
3. In the "Deploy & Run Transactions" tab, select "Injected Web3" as the environment.
4. Select the contract from the dropdown menu and click "Deploy".
5. In the "Transaction Details" modal, enter the necessary constructor arguments (name, symbol, decimals, and total supply) and click "Submit".

To interact with the front-end:
1. Connect the wallet (Metamask).
2. Set amount, to address and transact.
